Welcome to the Virtual Chamber Experience

An oasis of online virtual performances of your favorite chamber music!

On this website, you can find a myriad of online virtual performances of chamber music. Here you can find collaborations mainly from aspiring music students in North America. Ranging from composers such as Schubert, to even arrangements from students themselves, you'll be sure to have a good time!

Made specifically in the context of the COVID-19 pandemic, the goal of this website is to make it much easier to find all sorts of collaborations that are difficult to find unless searched for specifically. It is also designed for those who cannot go out much and experience classical music (primarily the elderly). You can also request a certain piece for a performance spefically made for you with a special, interactive element!



On this website, you can also apply to be part of a collaboration if you are a music student.
